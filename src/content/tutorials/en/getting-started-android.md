<section class="doc-lead-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Introduction</p>
  <p class="doc-lead">This guide explains how to download the OpenCloud app on Android, connect it to KM0 Cloud, and sign in.</p>
</section>

<section class="doc-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 1</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Download the app</h2>
  <ol class="doc-list">
    <li>Open Google Play Store on your Android device.</li>
    <li>Search for <strong>OpenCloud</strong>.</li>
    <li>Download and install the official application.</li>
  </ol>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">Direct link: <a href="https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=eu.opencloud.android" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">OpenCloud on Google Play</a>.</p>
</section>

<section class="doc-block doc-block-alt">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 2</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Configure the server when you open the app</h2>
  <ol class="doc-list">
    <li>Open OpenCloud for the first time.</li>
    <li>When prompted, enter the OpenCloud server address.</li>
    <li>Type the same URL you use in the browser: <code>https://cloud.km0digital.com/</code>.</li>
    <li>If the app offers a tap-to-copy option for the URL, use it to avoid typing mistakes.</li>
  </ol>
</section>

<section class="doc-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 3</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Continue to sign-in</h2>
  <ol class="doc-list">
    <li>After entering the URL, tap <strong>Next</strong> or the arrow icon (depending on the app version).</li>
    <li>You will see the sign-in screen with the same options as on the web: own account, Google, Apple, or others, depending on what the server supports.</li>
  </ol>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">For more detail on signing in, see the <a href="/en/tutorials/getting-started-web/">Getting started in your browser</a> tutorial.</p>
</section>

<section class="doc-block doc-block-alt">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Common problems</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Troubleshooting (Android and iOS)</h2>
  <p class="doc-block-intro"><strong>Error: "Wrong server address format"</strong></p>
  <ul class="doc-list">
    <li>Check that the URL is exactly the one provided by KM0 Cloud: <code>https://cloud.km0digital.com/</code>.</li>
    <li>Make sure there are no extra spaces at the beginning or end.</li>
    <li>Do not omit <code>https://</code> or use incorrect characters.</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">If the problem persists, contact KM0 support through the official channels.</p>
</section>

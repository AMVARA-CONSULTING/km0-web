<section class="doc-lead-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Introduction</p>
  <p class="doc-lead">This guide explains how to access KM0 Cloud from a web browser, starting from zero. No technical knowledge is required: just your usual browser and your credentials.</p>
</section>

<section class="doc-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 1</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Open the KM0 Cloud URL</h2>
  <ol class="doc-list">
    <li>Open your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, or another).</li>
    <li>Go to <a href="https://cloud.km0digital.com/">https://cloud.km0digital.com/</a>.</li>
    <li>If you are not signed in yet, the site will ask you to do so.</li>
  </ol>
</section>

<section class="doc-block doc-block-alt">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 2</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Sign-in options</h2>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">You can sign in in several ways:</p>
  <ul class="doc-list">
    <li><strong>KM0 Cloud account:</strong> email or username and password.</li>
    <li><strong>External providers:</strong> Google or Apple, if enabled on the server.</li>
    <li><strong>New registration:</strong> if you do not have an account yet, you can register from the same sign-in page.</li>
  </ul>
</section>

<section class="doc-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 3</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Sign in with Google or Apple</h2>
  <ol class="doc-list">
    <li>Tap the Google or Apple button on the sign-in screen.</li>
    <li>Select the account you want to use.</li>
    <li>Confirm permissions if your browser asks for them.</li>
  </ol>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">The process usually completes in a few seconds.</p>
</section>

<section class="doc-block doc-block-alt">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 4</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Register with your own account</h2>
  <ol class="doc-list">
    <li>Choose the registration option on the sign-in page.</li>
    <li>Enter your email address.</li>
    <li>Choose a username.</li>
    <li>Set a secure password, as you would on any other website.</li>
  </ol>
</section>

<section class="doc-block">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Step 5</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">After sign-in</h2>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">Once signed in, you will be redirected to your cloud space. From there you can upload files, share them, and collaborate with your team.</p>
</section>

<section class="doc-block doc-block-alt">
  <p class="doc-block-title">Common problems</p>
  <h2 class="doc-block-heading">Troubleshooting (web)</h2>
  <ul class="doc-list">
    <li><strong>I forgot my password:</strong> use the recovery option on the sign-in page.</li>
    <li><strong>The Google or Apple button does not appear:</strong> that method may not be enabled on your server; try your KM0 Cloud account instead.</li>
    <li><strong>The page does not load:</strong> check your internet connection and that the URL is exactly <code>https://cloud.km0digital.com/</code>.</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="doc-block-intro">If you use the mobile app and see server address errors, see the <a href="/en/tutorials/getting-started-android/">Android</a> or <a href="/en/tutorials/getting-started-ios/">iOS</a> tutorials.</p>
</section>
